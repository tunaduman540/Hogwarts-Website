About Hogwarts:
Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a renowned magical institution located in Scotland. Founded over a thousand years ago by the four greatest witches and wizards of the age, Hogwarts has served as a beacon of magical education, fostering the talents and abilities of young witches and wizards from all over the wizarding world.
Numbers of Students and Staff:
  • Students: Hogwarts typically enrolls around 1,000 students each year, spanning the ages of 11 to 18. These students are sorted into one of four houses upon arrival: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin.
  • Staff: The teaching staff at Hogwarts consists of a diverse group of witches and wizards, each specializing in different subjects of magical study. Led by the Headmaster or Headmistress, the staff is dedicated to providing a well-rounded education to the students and nurturing their magical talents.
  • Headmaster's Office: The Headmaster or Headmistress is the chief administrator of Hogwarts and oversees all aspects of the school's operation
  • Deputy Head's Office: Assists the Headmaster in administrative duties and serves as a liaison between the staff and students.
  • Heads of Houses: Each of the four Hogwarts houses has a designated Head of House who is responsible for the well-being and academic progress of the students in their house.
  • Department of Magical Education: Responsible for curriculum development, teacher training, and ensuring that Hogwarts meets the educational standards set forth by the Ministry of Magic
  • Department of Magical Maintenance: Oversees the upkeep and maintenance of Hogwarts Castle and grounds, ensuring a safe and magical environment for students and staff.
  • Department of Magical Security: Handles matters of magical security within Hogwarts, including protection against dark forces and the enforcement of school rules and regulations.
  • Department of Magical Events and Activities: Plans and organizes various events, activities, and extracurricular programs to enhance the student experience at Hogwarts, such as Quidditch matches, the Yule Ball, and field trips.
