CAMPUS LIFE
Faculty Description
Faculty of Charms Teaches students various charms and spells for everyday use and defense.
Faculty of Transfiguration Focuses on the art of transfiguration, changing the form or appearance of objects.
Faculty of Potions Instructs students in the brewing of magical potions with various effects.
Faculty of Defense Against the Dark Arts Trains students to defend themselves against dark creatures and curses.
Institute Description
Institute of Magical Law Focuses on magical law, including the study of wizarding legal systems and regulations.
Institute of Wizarding Medicine Provides education in magical healing and medical practices for wizards and magical creatures.
Institute of Wizarding Arts Offers courses in various magical arts, including music, dance, and visual arts.
Institute of Magical Research Conducts research in various fields of magic, pushing the boundaries of magical knowledge.
School Description
School of Auror Training Prepares students for careers as Aurors, skilled wizards who combat dark forces and enforce magical law.
School of Potioneering Specializes in the art and science of potion-making, training students for careers as potion masters and apothecaries.
School of Magical Engineering Instructs students in the brewing of magical potions with various effects.
School of Magical Horticulture Trains students in the cultivation and care of magical plants for various purposes, including potion ingredients and magical creature habitats.
